Classique Entertainment

The Classique Identity

If you could be the god of your own world, what would you do?

Classique Entertainment is an entertainment designed to give space for the older coverist community. Everyone in Classique is older than 20, making a unique space for adults in the community to be able to express their hobbies and grow their abilities.

We have fun lore that you can opt-in as much as you please, voice acting, cover groups, possibly some original music, and opportunities to learn and practice staff roles. We also have casual collabs and a space to hangout if you don't want to deal with lore or ent stuff.

We accept all people 23+, with case-by-case consideration of 20-22-year-olds.

Gaea

"If you had the opportunity to live again, would you take it?"

Gaea (가이아) is an upcoming online K-pop cover group loosely based on greek mythology with a small twist in mind. Our story starts with the children of Gaea, Goddess of Earth. Upset and close to their deaths, they begin to lose hope, the Titans already at a disadvantage since they disobeyed their father, Uranus. Their fate lies in the hands of their mother, Gaea, who by the way, promises the children a chance to start over if they carry out a somewhat simple task for her. Becoming idols. They are to gather a large amount of attention from the mortals on earth and only then will they return to their rightful place as the Gods and Goddesses of Mt. Olympus.

Roles: Vocalist.
