SAE Entertainment

It's a title in process and honestly SAE is just a placeholder that definitely needs to be re-named, but this is my idea for a lore driven idol group. The story follows the American based idol group as they go from rookie to idol. The twist? There's a murder mystery aspect to it. Idols and trainees keep on getting into freak accidents and dying, and the main characters are quite worried.

At a glance

TW: Death. Characters are killed, and much of the supporting cast are dead idols. And because the conflict involves the death of trainees, this means teens are killed.

Premise:

Idols and trainees of SA Entertainment (SAE) have been dying since the company started gaining traction. The newest idols of the company, *E, get visited by a strange, supernatural phenomenon that grants them the ability to do something and save their peers from premature deaths. (They get supernatural powers.)

What's holding me back?

  1. I worry that this is too heavy for an idol group. Idol groups tend to be very light hearted and cheery, the dark nature of this concept might be too dark for an idol group.
  2. Because it's such a big project, I would need at least one co-leader. Having someone to help with the logistics would allow me to focus on developing the story so the plot can keep moving.
  3. Aesthetics. These are all original characters and because they have a story, how are we going to show that story? Making a comic or an animation would be a lot of work, so where's a medium that isn't a podcast?
